# Letters of Valisthea

This font family is inspired by the **Valisthean alphabet** featured in **FINAL FANTASY XVI**. It captures the mystical and ancient aesthetic of the game and reimagines it into a functional typeface.

## 🖋️ Font Styles

### 1. LoV-Display
A bold typeface specifically designed for **headlines and titles**.
- **Supported Characters**: Uppercase (A-Z), Numbers (0-9)

### 2. LoV-Script
A **handwritten style** font suitable for body text and decorative purposes.
- **Supported Characters**: Uppercase & Lowercase (A-Z), Numbers (0-9)
- **Punctuation & Symbols**: Supports various marks including — (Em Dash), – (En Dash), - (Hyphen), " , ' , ; , : , , , .
